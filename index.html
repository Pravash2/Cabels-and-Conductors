who are we?

GEETANSH CABLES & CONDUCTORS Private Ltd. is an industrial company that manufactures AB Cables, AAC, AAAC Conductors & Service Wires. GEETANSH has a committed approach to provide superior, world-class and quality products, ensure customer satisfaction by providing top-notch service. Technological innovation powered by an entrepreneurial drive, backed by a dedicated work-force and supported by significant capital investment, GEETANSH is on its way to carving a special niche for itself.GEETANSH employs a wide array of state-of-art industrial technologies in its manufacturing units- ultra modern RBD Machines, Extruders, Tubular Stranding Machines etc.
