GEETANSH CABLES & CONDUCTORS Private Ltd. is an industrial company that manufactures AB Cables, AAC, AAAC Conductors & Service Wires. GEETANSH has a committed approach to provide superior, world-class and quality products, ensure customer satisfaction by providing top-notch service. Technological innovation powered by an entrepreneurial drive, backed by a dedicated work-force and supported by significant capital investment, GEETANSH is on its way to carving a special niche for itself.GEETANSH employs a wide array of state-of-art industrial technologies in its manufacturing units- ultra modern RBD Machines, Extruders, Tubular Stranding Machines etc.

AB Cables or Aerial Bundled Cables are an innovative system design for Over Head (OH) power distribution system. GEETANSH manufactures AB Cables that are highly cost-effective, reliable & the insulation has been developed to withstand heat, cold & intense sunlight. Our AB Cables provide higher safety & reliability, lower power losses, stability in voltage regulation, thus enhancing the overall power system economy by reducing installation, maintenance & operation costs as well as minimizing distribution losses.

All Aluminium Conductors (AAC) , also known as Aluminium Stranded Conductors are used in Over Head transmission & distribution power lines.GEETANSH manufactures AAC Cables from excellent quality Aluminium wire strands that are highly resistant to corrosion.

AAAC or All Aluminium Alloy Conductors are widely used for primary & secondary transmission in bare Over Head (OH) transmission & distribution lines- generally ranging from 11KV-800KV & High Voltage (HV) substations. Our AAAC Cables are usable in highly polluted industrial areas & coastal regions due to corrosion resistance. They have high electrical conductivity & better strength-to-weight ratio.

All our high performance AB Cables, AAC, AAAC & Service Wires are stringently tested on various parameters before despatch.

Our Founder
Mr. Ayashkant Mohanty
Founder of GEETANSH CABLES, GEETA ASSOCIATES

Mr. Ayashkant Mohanty is the founder of GEETANSH CABLES, GEETA ASSOCIATES & is also on the Board of Directors of Sunshine Hospital, Bhubaneswar.

He served at the highest ranks during his 12 year old illustrious career at POLYCABS

He held very powerful positions during his 12-year old illustrious career in POLYCAB- one of India’s leading manufacturers of cables, wires & allied products.

Mr. Ayashkant served as the Country-Head-India, Coaxial Cables Division, from 2001-2005. From 2005 June-2013 September, he was appointed as the Deputy General Manager, Odisha- for the entire POLYCAB Product Division (Both HT & LT).

In May 2007, while serving at POLYCAB, he decided to start his own cable & wire trading venture, GEETA ASSOCIATES in Bhubaneswar – which turned out to be a huge success. The presence of many spurious, low-quality cables in the market propelled him to think out-of-the-box, thus leading him to start his second venture- GEETANSH CABLES in November 2020.

GEETANSH CABLES is a cable manufacturing plant located in Bhadrak with state-of-art technology founded with a vision to provide the best quality electrical cables at very affordable rates.

During the same time period, in November 2020, he was appointed as the one of the Directors at Sunshine Hospital- a state-of-art multi-specialty hospital in Bhubaneswar.

Mr. Ayashkant is also deeply committed to giving back to the society. He, along with some of his friends started a small venture where they sponsor the entire educational expenses of 40 blind students every year from Ranipatna Blind School, Balasore. These blind children have now gone on to study at some of the most prestigious institutes in India. He is also a generous donor to the Chief Minister’s Relief Fund during natural calamities.
